Janken   Impact

By Vincent Ignatius S. Amante

Based off a minigame inside the classic social game 'Club Penguin' comes Janken Impact!

A card game thematically inspired by popular game 'Genshin Impact', and mechanically by 'Card Jitsu'

This is a game akin to rock-paper-scissors, where you pick one element, and there's a chance that the enemy will pick an element that loses to you, beats you, or is the same as yours!

In the case of two matching elements, the higher number will win!

While its predecessors rely more on luck to play the game, Janken Impact offers you the opportunity to see your opponent's cards.
Giving you the chance to strategise the best possible outcome!



How to Play

Controls

The game is played completely through clicks.

When there is no card on the field, click any of the cards on your deck at the bottom to play it into the field.

When the round is over (when cards are on the field), click anywhere inside the canvas to start a new round.

Cards and Rounds

A single match is composed of rounds. In each round, a card chosen by both players will be pitted against the other

Each player will be granted 5 cards, with a randomised element and a randomised power level.
All cards are visible, and only one card from each deck will be chosen.

The chosen card will be discarded after the round, and a new randomised card will take its place.

Winning

The winning card will grant the owner a point as its corresponding Elemental Win. If it is the first time the player won with that element, another point is awarded as a Unique Win.

A winner is decided when a player has either gotten 3 Elemental Wins, or 3 Unique Wins.

Elementals and Reactions

Currently, there are only three elements in the game: Pyro (fire), Cryo (ice), and Hydro.

All three elements are strong against one element, but weak against another.

The card with the stronger element automatically wins the round, but if pitted against its own element, the card with the higher number wins instead.

Element Chart with Reactions

Pyro melts Cryo, which freezes Hydro, which then douses Pyro.

Have fun with Janken Impact!
